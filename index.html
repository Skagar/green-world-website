About:

Green World is an environmental platform that seeks to protect the environment against the misuse or degradation from human forces. In Malaysia, it established itself as one of the fastest-growing NGOs in the field, with plans for operation in middle east and countries such as China, United States, Singapore, HongKong and India. The organization aims to be the ultimate platform that aims to make a difference in the world today . "We work hard to uphold this promise and ensure this spirit is instilled in everything we do daily"

Mission And Vision:

Green World Organization (GWO) aims in redeveloping the biological ecosystem of earth by various methods which will be designed keeping in mind the needs and well being of each and every individual. Some of our missions and visions which we aim to achieve in the upcoming years are:

  • Community based reforestation and eco agriculture.
  • Educating society about issues related to our ecosystem .
  • Adapting appropriate technologies such as "green charcoal" to reduce tree cutting; rainwater catchment and drip irrigation systems.
  • Reducing earth's natural temperature by Controlling effect of Global Warming.
  • Protection of wildlife.

Activities:

Green world as an environmental Organization not only works tirelessly with its volunteers for the well being of the nature but also promotes the same among each and every individual.Along with our main aim that is to improve the condition of our ecosystem , we also encourage people to contribute their part in changing the world.We do so by conducting webinars,seminars,plantation drives etc. in different cities inside as well as outside the country.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

IS THIS A NON-PROFITABLE ORGANIZATION?

Yes, this is a non-profitable organization and works from the funds received from people.

WHO STARTED THE GREEN WORLD INITIATIVE?

We are a group of passionate people who strongly believe in the theory of six degrees of separation that people are 6 steps apart to save the world from the six degrees of extinction. Our mission is to bring the world closer, making it a better and greener place to live in.

WHAT CAN I DO TO HELP?

It’s easy: Sign up for our mailing list, stay informed of our activities and volunteer to help! It’s that simple. We will be rolling out a series of activities and campaigns based on the Six Degrees of Separation concept, that we hope you could be a part of!

WHAT IS THE CONCEPT OF SIX DEGREES OF SEPARATION ABOUT?

It is an innovative social experiment founded on the idea that anyone and anything in the world is connected through six or fewer steps. We want to utilize the concept’s core idea to create a social linkage, on which an environmentally conscious message can be sent out to all people in the world.
